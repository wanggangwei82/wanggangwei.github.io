Research interests

My research interests are in the combinatorial properties of algebraic structures associated with Coxeter groups (for example, Kazhdan-Lusztig theory) and enumerative properties of combinatorial structures (for example, trees, lattice paths, set partitions). Recent researches are concentrated mainly on the combinatorial properties of Schubert polynomials.
