I am an assistant professor at Tianjin Normal University. I am currently a visiting scholar at the University of Pennsylvania working with James Haglund from August 2018 to August 2019. I received my Ph.D. in June 2015 from Nankai University under the guidance of Arthur L.B. Yang.
