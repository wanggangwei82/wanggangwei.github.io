 I am an instructor at the Tianjin Normal University. I am currently a visiting scholar at the University of Pennsylvania where my mentor is [James Haglund](https://www.math.upenn.edu/~jhaglund/) from August 2018 to August 2019.  I received my Ph.D. in June 2015 from Nankai University under the guidance of [Arthur L.B. Yang](http://www.combinatorics.net.cn/homepage/yang/).
